Zixuan Ke

I am a PhD student at the University of Illinois, Chicago, where I work on Continual Learning and Natural Language Processing. I am advised by Prof. Bing Liu and was advised by Prof. Vincent Ng at the University of Texas, Dallas. I have closely worked with Hu Xu from Meta AI and Lei Shu from Google Research. During my Ph.D., I worked as a research intern at Meta AI, Amazon AI and Tencent AI Lab.

My research so far has studied Language Model Post-training (Domain-adaptive Pre-training / Pre-finetuning), Continual Learning in NLP, Continual Learning in Image Classification, and Argument Mining.
